Our Past Experiences

At Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), our cultural initiatives have consistently celebrated creativity, unity, and student talent.


The "RGPV Jammers Live" event during Cultural Fest'24 showcased electrifying musical performances, reflecting the vibrant spirit of our campus community.

Events like "Udaan 2.0" provided a platform for architectural innovation and student-led exhibitions, highlighting the ingenuity of our students.


Through these events, we've fostered an environment where cultural expression thrives, and students are empowered to share their passions with the broader community.
